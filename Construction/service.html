Our Services

Alliston Sand & Gravel offers a variety of delivery services based on customer preference and quantity ordered. These options include Tandem Dump, Roto Dump, Triaxle Dump, and Stone Slinger.

Delivery is available in the general area of Highway 10 to the west, Highway 27 to the east, County Road 21 to the north, and Highway 9 to the south.

Emergency service hours are also available upon request. Delivery charges are determined by the distance and quantity ordered.

• Tandem Dump – 15 tons
• Roto Dump – 15 tons
• Triaxle Dump – 22.5 tons
• Stone Slinger – 22.5 tons
